Great class - just 6 assignments all with reworks!
This class is pretty chill and had some interesting content. A few of the sets were a bit tricky at times (perhaps due to me not going to lectures), but overall the work was very reasonable and the structure of the class is forgiving. In general, I would recommend taking courses with Michael Vanier as the classes are well structured, have interesting content, and have a fair workload.
It's a nice course! Prof. Vanier is very engaging lecturer, and also very understanding; I managed to work with him to finish all my assignments and pass the course (yay!), despite really struggling with burnout from my other classes this term.
I found the course material interesting enough; if you've taken CS 4, it's kind of like a next level version of that. Difficulty-wise, it's a bit more challenging than CS 4; I managed to mostly avoid having to go to office hours in CS 4, also thanks to having other friends taking the class at the time, who could help me with things I didn't understand. In this class I ended up going to office hours quite a few times, which was really helpful for working out the little parts I'd get stuck in sometimes. (Especially since I didn't have anyone else taking the class with me, this time.) So, if you get stuck that's a good idea. Still, it's nothing overwhelming like some of the other programming-heavy courses here, and again, the instructor and TAs are very helpful.
General notes:
(1) You don't have to go to lectures, but they're pretty nice. (And less effort than trying to catch up on the slides on your own, which is doable if you are desperate, but speaking from experience, I would not recommend it.) Also, Prof. Vanier put cute animal pictures up for us, which I liked way better than the AI generated pictures we had in CS21. (Personal taste.)
(2) The whole class is wrapped up in 6 assignments, no midterm, no final. This means that sometimes there will be a few weeks between due dates, so if for example you happened to be taking a course like CS38 at the same time, the extra breathing room will feel really nice.
(3) Reworks! Yay.
All in all, would recommend 👍👍👍
