Would definitely recommend this class, and I hope it is taught in the future. I always wanted to learn how to use a pico, and I accomplished this goal. You also get to build up to a cool final project (I built a small light-following robot). You'll do equal parts coding and building/wiring. It is very useful to go to OH for the labs/hw, but they're not extremely intensive (1-3 hours). Lecture is also very helpful and not boring which is great. The 3-4 homework's are done in groups of 2-3 students. The instructor and TAs this year were very understanding and helpful. Next year it would be cool if there was more funding available for different parts.

Neil was really good during OH at explaining concepts even thought I skipped his lecture lol. Great CS 12 very rewarding.
