This course is well-structured. There are 9 sets with one individual question on each and no finals, midterms, or quizzes.
You have one free late set for the class which is nice. You have a set every week (9 total) but no midterm or final. The last set is shorter than the rest. If you haven't taken logic before, the material can be challenging but with office hours and the lecture notes the homework sets are doable. I chose to take this class instead of CS21 and I am very happy with that decision.
