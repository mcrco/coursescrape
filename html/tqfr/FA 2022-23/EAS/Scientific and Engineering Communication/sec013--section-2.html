I thought the homework were well thought out. I think it was nice that I could incorporate my research into the homework, as that was a tangible way that I was benefitting from this class. I would say the homework is usually a little under 2 hours of work a week, so that is nice. I haven't taken the other SEC classes, but I would recommend SEC 13 to fulfill the SEC 11/12/13 requirement.

Prof. Bickford is a very nice and engaging professor. I feel like this course would've been really helpful to have taken as a frosh or earlier in my academic career. However, I do like the main takeaway of the course (which the professor reiterates very well)--you should always tell a story, even if you're discussing a complicated technical topic.
