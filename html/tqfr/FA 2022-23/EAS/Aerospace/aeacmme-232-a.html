Do you have any constructive comments for other students considering taking this course? Tough course but that is complete and rewarding. Get a buddy to compare your work, start HWs early and go to OH.
Prof. Bae's OH are wonderful; she will take the time to clarify topics to make sure you know what is going on. We had 4 homework sets (4 x 15%) and 1 final project (40%). The last problem of each homework set will ensure that you are making progress in your project. The homework problems are fulfilling to solve. The textbook is helpful for some of the more algebra-intensive problems, but most of the problems are closely related to the lectures, so attend them!
