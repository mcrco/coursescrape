ME133B was an interesting introduction to localization, mapping and planning for robotics. The assignments were aways well-thought through and organized. Each assignment involved coding (Python), but rarely from scratch, so most of the difficulty was conceptual. There were weekly assignments, and two quizzes, with no midterm. There was also a Final Project in the last two weeks of term, similar to ME133A. Gunter as always put together a stimulating robotics course, and I would recommend that anyone looking for a robotics advanced elective take this class.
