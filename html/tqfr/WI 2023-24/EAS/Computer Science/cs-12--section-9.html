What would you tell a student thinking about taking this course in the future?

It was a good introduction to microcontrollers, using a Raspberry Pi Pico. We got to design our own project during the final month, which was fun to make. This class has unfortunately made my wallet a little lighter because now I want to make my own projects after this class, and we don't get to keep the parts :(

Not a lot of workload, overall I recommend this if you're completely new to microcontrollers.
Pretty interesting and manageable introduction to microcontrollers. However, I've had some experience with microcontrollers and I don't think I learned that much during the course's labs. I think the cool part of the course is the final project, where you can do pretty much anything.
Take it if you haven't worked with microcontrollers (Raspberry pi pico, Arduino) during highschool. This course will help you learn it in a shorter period of time, with less time commitment.
