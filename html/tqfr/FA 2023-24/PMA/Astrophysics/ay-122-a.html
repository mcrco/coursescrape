Comments

What would you tell a student thinking about taking this course in the future?

I took the course with Chuck Steidel and Andrew Howard. The course is not hard, but teaches a lot. You will get to know the basic principles of optical/IR instrumentation and data analysis in a very practical way. I would strongly recommend this course to anyone interested in astronomy and astrophysics.
Some weeks may be 0 work and other weeks will be double work due to chance and when labs need to be moved to. So you'll have to have some flexibility in your schedule for this. It's a good class and I recommend it though.
