Comments

What would you tell a student thinking about taking this course in the future?

This course was overall good. As an applied physics I found some presentations better than others (floquet engineering, condensed matter physics, etc.). There were a couple presentations that made me interested in topics that I had never thought about (ex: dimensionless numbers). Note that this is one of the few classes that still has lecture during finals week. Overall if you have 2h per week to spend on listening to presentations on physics take this course.
I would highly recommend this class for anyone interested in physics or wants to learn more about the current research going on.
This was an interesting class! You get a lot of exposure to different physics research projects.
If you're at all interested in physics, I would highly recommend this course! It is engaging and fun, and, while the 2 hour lectures may seem daunting, it is so worth it!
A lot of fun field trips/lab tours, Prof. Spiropulu is very good, make sure to take notes while you are at the talks because that can help a lot with understanding and retention.
