What would you tell a student thinking about taking this course in the future?

The interviews version of CS 12 is a great class for CS majors getting into what it takes to get an internship. The TAs are super helpful and provide lots of chances to do one-on-one practice interviews but also puts a large emphasis on Leetcode practice. Not much of a commitment in terms of time or work. I think I actually did nothing for three weeks within the term since I had so much work in other classes and I still passed. They have short lectures on important coding topics like sorting too.
It's just doing leetcode pretty much. There were some interesting talks about interview tips in addition. Easy 3 units.
this was such a great course if you want to do Leetcode and prepare for technical interviews
