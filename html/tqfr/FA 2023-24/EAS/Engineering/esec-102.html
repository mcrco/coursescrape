Comments

What would you tell a student thinking about taking this course in the future?

Take it!
This is truly an incredible class. If you are at all interested in entrepreneurship and have the opportunity to take it, you should seriously do so. For me at least, this is probably one of the most useful classes I have taken at Caltech. And not only that, but it was also one of the most enjoyable. Bill is so passionate and knowledgeable about what he is teaching that you cannot help but feel inspired. The assignments are very well crafted to be genuinely useful both for the final project, which is a presentation + supplementary written document outlining a company you create, and real-world entrepreneurship. He also provides excellent resources, including his own advice and consultation whenever necessary. I could write an essay on how great this course is and all the reasons I loved it, but alas this is just a TQFR. If you are interested in business/startups/VC, trust me and take this course. Even though it is a non-trivial amount of work, I do not think you will regret it.
Highly recommend to anyone interested in entrepreneurship / VC investment.
