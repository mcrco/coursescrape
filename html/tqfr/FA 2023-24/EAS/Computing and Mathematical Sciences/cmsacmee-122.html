Comments

What would you tell a student thinking about taking this course in the future?

It is a hard course, and the grading is harsh. However, it is also a very interesting course. The lectures are done well, most of the homework is enlightening in same way or another (and the parts that aren't usually aren't too bad), the pacing is good, and the material is just downright interesting. The lecture notes are not online, so you do have to go to class, but class time does not feel like it is wasted. If you want to learn about convex optimization, this is definitely a good course to do it.
Make sure to review lots of linear algebra, ensure your outside workload for the quarter is not heavy as this class is very time consuming
Budget at least 20 hours for this course. The course is more about convex analysis than optimization. A strong foundation on linear algebra is a must.
This is a challenging course but you will get out what you put in. If you take the time to struggle through the homeworks on your own, you will find yourself with a very strong base in optimization that prepares you well for future classes and research.
